# Ingredients #

* 1 3/4 cups flour
* 2 tsp baking powder
* 1/4 teaspoon salt
* 3/4 tsp salt
* 1/3 cup butter
* 2/3 cup sugar
* 2 eggs
* 1 cup mashed bananas
* 1/2 cup nuts

# Directions #

Cream together butter and sugar, add eggs and beat until light and fluffy.
Sift dry ingredients together.
Add alternately with banana mixture to egg mixture and mix well.
Add nuts.
Pour into greased bread pan and bake at 350Â° for 50 minutes.

Makes one loaf.
