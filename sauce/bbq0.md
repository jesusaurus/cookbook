## BBQ 0

#### Ingredients

* 1 Tbsp butter
* 1 cup bourbon
* 1 small onion (minced)
* 4 cloves garlic (pressed)
* 12 oz tomato puree
* 1 cup ketchup
* 1/3 cup A1
* 1/2 cup red wine vinegar
* 2/3 cup brown sugar
* 1 Tbsp salt
* 1 tsp white pepper
* 1 tsp Sriracha

#### Directions

In a double boiler, melt butter in bourbon and saut√© onion and garlic.
Stir in tomato puree, ketchup, A1, and vinegar over medium heat.
Add brown sugar, salt, pepper, and Sriracha to double boiler mix.
Simmer.
