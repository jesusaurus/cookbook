## Fancy Meatloaf

#### Ingredients

* 1 lbs ground beef
* 1 cup bread crumbs
* 2 eggs
* 1 carrot (grated)
* 1 onion (diced)
* 15 oz black beans
* 1/3 cup cream
* 1 tsp chili powder
* 1 tsp cumin
* 1/2 tsp cinnamon
* 1/4 cup [Meatloaf Sauce](../sauce/meatloaf.md)

#### Directions

Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Combine all ingredients by hand until well mixed,
then put into a greased loaf pan. Pour the sauce on top of the loaf and
bake for 1 hour, then allow to cool.
